Deep Dive

Why was Vector built?

Most GPA tools are built for SEO and ads. Vector was engineered for the student. No tracking, no accounts, and no friction. Just a high-performance calculation engine that respects your data and your time.

Privacy?

Your grades never leave your browser. We use LocalStorage to sync your data locally. Total privacy by design.

Global Ready

From the 4.0 US system to the 10.0 International scale—Vector adapts to your region instantly. One engine, zero boundaries.

How do weights work?

Vector applies a standard +1.0 boost for AP/IB courses and a +0.5 boost for Honors. This allows you to track your weighted GPA alongside your unweighted stats in real-time.
